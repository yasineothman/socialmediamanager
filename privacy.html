Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Social Media Manager collects and processes the following information:

  • Social media account tokens (Instagram, TikTok) that you provide for authentication
  • Content you upload or schedule for posting (videos, captions, hashtags)
  • Basic account information from connected platforms (username, profile stats)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the Service:

  • Publishing and scheduling content to your connected social media accounts
  • Displaying analytics and engagement metrics from your accounts
  • Managing your content calendar and post queue

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your credentials or content to any servers other than the official Instagram and TikTok APIs required to publish your content.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service interacts with:

  • Instagram Graph API (Meta) - to publish content and retrieve analytics
  • TikTok Content Posting API - to publish videos and retrieve metrics

These platforms have their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to any third parties beyond what is necessary to provide the Service through the APIs listed above.

6. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete your data at any time by removing the application and its associated database files from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us via GitHub.
